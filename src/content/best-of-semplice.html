Challenge

Build a one-page website that showcased the best portfolios that were submitted to Semplice in 2016.

Get absolute positioning to work well within a responsive design.

Solution

This design initially seemed like a challenge to optimize for responsiveness, but I came up with the idea of creating a div that ran down the center of the length of the page to use as an anchor point. This way everything could be positioned according to the anchor div, which was always in the center of the page, regardless of screen size. I was able to use percentage positioning instead of pixels.
